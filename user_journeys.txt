Artist: See all but must sign in to create, edit and delete a song. Must sign in to create comment, edit one's own comment or delete it.
Artist can also choose to customise the pages. 
Must also have the authority to delete a comment that belongs to their song.

Listener: Can access everything but can not create, edit or delete a song. Can create, edit or delete a comment.

Moderator: Can not create, edit or delete a song. Can delete a comment.
